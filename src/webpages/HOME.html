The E-Church System

E-Church system was made to manage various church services in a single platform with ease in communication and administration. It assists the churches and other religious organizations in organization and automation of daily operations. The system usually assist in the management of membership, messages, events, accounting, and church transactions where you can request different kinds of certificates and services like mass service, marriage certificate, baptismal certificate, and many more. With the help of technology, churches will consume less time for their operations and engage more to the growth of their fellowship.

Member Management

Keep track of you member's information. From attendance tracking and even to handling church groups.

Membership

Include photos and information about yourself. Has a membership features that keeps track of church groups and attendance.
